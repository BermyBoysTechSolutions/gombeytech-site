## Build objective
Build a premium website for **Gombey Tech LLC**, a **web development agency** focused on high-end websites, landing pages, and redesigns for businesses that need to look more credible, premium, and conversion-focused online.

This site will be used in **cold email outreach**, so it must immediately build trust and make business owners think:
**“If their own site looks like this, they can build mine.”**

## Primary CTA
- Main CTA: **Book a Call**
- Calendly: `https://calendly.com/ianinho`

The user should have a clear CTA available throughout the page, not just at the bottom.

## Positioning
Position Gombey Tech LLC primarily around:
- Web Development
- Website Redesigns
- Landing Pages
- Premium business websites

Automation can be mentioned subtly as a secondary advanced capability, but the homepage should primarily sell **web development**.

Do **not** position the brand as a generic software development agency.

## Audience
The site should feel broad enough for any serious business owner, but naturally resonate with aesthetics-sensitive industries like:
- med spas
- cosmetic surgery clinics
- dental practices

Do not make the website feel niche-locked to only those categories.

## Brand direction
Use the provided logo as the main brand anchor.

The company has Bermudian inspiration, but that influence should remain **subtle**. Do not make the site feel old-world, heraldic, governmental, or tourist-themed. Let the identity live mostly through the logo, confidence, and originality.

## Visual direction
Create something that feels:
- premium
- modern
- sharp
- elegant
- immersive
- high-trust
- visually expensive

Avoid generic AI agency design and template-looking layouts.
Avoid dark futuristic cheese.
If using darker tones, keep them refined and premium.

## Motion / interaction
Use a lot of **tasteful premium animation**, including:
- refined page-load reveals
- scroll-based motion
- hover interactions
- layered or parallax depth
- animated cards and mockups
- smooth section transitions
- premium microinteractions

The motion should feel expensive and controlled, not gimmicky or chaotic.

## Homepage sections
Build a homepage with sections such as:
- Hero
- Trust / authority strip
- Services
- Why Gombey
- Featured work
- Process
- Optional advanced capabilities
- Strong closing CTA

## Featured work
Subtly feature:
- NamazLock
- Bermy Banana
- polished concept work

Present these as selected work / featured builds in a premium way. Do not make fake-looking loud claims.

## Copy direction
Write copy that is:
- confident
- concise
- premium
- clear
- slightly blunt

Avoid generic agency fluff.
The site should feel like it was built for serious business owners who care about perception, trust, and conversion.

## Conversion constraints
This is an outreach-driven website. Every major section should include a visible CTA or CTA-adjacent action.

Include CTA opportunities in:
- hero
- after services
- after featured work
- after process
- navigation
- final CTA section

If it fits the design, use a tasteful sticky or persistent CTA.

The visitor should quickly understand:
- these people are legit
- they can clearly build premium work
- they can make my business look better
- I know what to do next

## Final quality bar
Do not produce a template-looking site.
Do not produce AI slop.
Make it feel handcrafted, animated, conversion-focused, and hard to replicate.
